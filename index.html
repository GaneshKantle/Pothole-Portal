About Us


PatchMyPath is a community-driven platform dedicated to making roads safer and more accessible. By empowering citizens to report potholes in real-time, we’re bridging the gap between road users and repair authorities. Our mission is simple: enable timely pothole repairs, reduce accidents, and improve the overall driving experience.

With features like easy pothole reporting, repair tracking, and data-driven road repair analytics, PatchMyPath brings transparency and efficiency to the road maintenance process. Using advanced sensor technology, our platform can also detect pothole depth and assess road conditions. This innovation allows for more accurate repair prioritization and faster responses.

We believe in the power of technology and community action to enhance road safety. By joining PatchMyPath, you’re helping to create smoother, safer roads for everyone. Let’s work together to reduce road hazards, one pothole at a time.
